    ▸ Finviz → Watchlist
    1. Drag the green button up to your browser's bookmarks bar.
    2. Open your screen on Finviz — use an Open in Finviz link above, or your own saved screen. Any view is fine.
    3. While that Finviz tab is showing, click the bookmark (not on this page — it only works on Finviz). A panel appears with the full list — hit Copy or Download.

    Fair-use note: this pages through Finviz's free results automatically, which their robots.txt asks bots to avoid. Keep it to occasional personal use — it runs gently (~one page every ½ second) in your own logged-in browser. For heavy or fully-sanctioned automation, Finviz Elite's export API is the proper route.

    Name it to match the watchlist.
    Prepend EXCHANGE: to every ticker without one. Off by default.
    Only correct if the whole list sits on one exchange. Your F13 / VAX screens mix NYSE & NASDAQ, so leave this off — bare US tickers resolve on their own, and any exchange found in a pasted CSV is kept.
    Verified against TradingView's help centre: upload wants a .txt file, symbols comma-separated, with an EXCHANGE:TICKER prefix recommended (bare US tickers usually resolve too). Hard cap is 1,000 symbols per watchlist — split anything larger.
    Import path in TradingView: click the watchlist name in the right toolbar → Import list… → pick your .txt.
