Finviz → TradingView

Paste messy Finviz screener output — a Tickers-view block, a plain list, or several pages at once. Get one clean comma-separated line, ready to save as a .txt and upload to a TradingView watchlist.

How to use Start here
One-time setup: in the Auto-collect panel below, drag the green ▸ Finviz → Watchlist button up onto your browser's bookmarks bar. You only ever do this once.

Every time — from screen to watchlist

  1. Open your screen on Finviz — click an Open in Finviz link in box 1, or open your own saved screen.
  2. On that Finviz page, click your Finviz → Watchlist bookmark. A small panel appears with every ticker — click Copy.
  3. In TradingView, open the watchlist you want to update.
  4. Clear it first: click the ⋯ menu at the top-right of the watchlist → Clear the list. Adding symbols appends to a list rather than replacing it, so clear it (or use a fresh, empty list) to avoid mixing in last week's names.
  5. Click + Add symbol, then paste (⌘V) the list you copied. Every ticker drops in at once — no file to download or upload.

No bookmarklet? Paste method

On Finviz, switch the view to Tickers, copy the block, paste it into box 2 below, and press Copy list — then do steps 3–5 above. (Downloading a .txt and using TradingView's Import list… still works too, if you prefer files.)

    Skip the copy-paste. Drag this button to your bookmarks bar once; then, on your Finviz screener page, click it — it pages through every result, de-dupes, and shows the finished list ready to Copy or Download.

    1. Drag the green button up to your browser's bookmarks bar.
    2. Open your screen on Finviz — use an Open in Finviz link above, or your own saved screen. Any view is fine.
    3. While that Finviz tab is showing, click the bookmark (not on this page — it only works on Finviz). A panel appears with the full list — hit Copy or Download.

    Fair-use note: this pages through Finviz's free results automatically, which their robots.txt asks bots to avoid. Keep it to occasional personal use — it runs gently (~one page every ½ second) in your own logged-in browser. For heavy or fully-sanctioned automation, Finviz Elite's export API is the proper route.

    Options

    Name it to match the watchlist.
    Prepend EXCHANGE: to every ticker without one. Off by default.
    Only correct if the whole list sits on one exchange. Your F13 / VAX screens mix NYSE & NASDAQ, so leave this off — bare US tickers resolve on their own, and any exchange found in a pasted CSV is kept.
    Verified against TradingView's help centre: upload wants a .txt file, symbols comma-separated, with an EXCHANGE:TICKER prefix recommended (bare US tickers usually resolve too). Hard cap is 1,000 symbols per watchlist — split anything larger.
    To refresh a watchlist (replace it with a new screen): open the list → click the ⋯ menu (top-right) → Clear the list first — importing adds symbols, it doesn't replace them. Then click the watchlist name → Import list… → pick your .txt. Or just import into a brand-new empty list.
